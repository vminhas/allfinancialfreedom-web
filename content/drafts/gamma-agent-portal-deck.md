Create a polished, professional slideshow presentation for a company called **All Financial Freedom**. This is being presented LIVE to our team of insurance and financial services agents to walk them through the new Agent Portal we built for them.

## BRANDING (apply to every slide)

- **Primary colors:** Deep navy (#142D48) backgrounds, gold (#C9A96E) accent text, headings, and buttons. White (#FFFFFF) body text. Light blue-gray (#9BB0C4) for secondary text.
- **Fonts:** Use a serif font (like Cormorant Garamond or Playfair Display) for slide titles and headlines. Use a clean sans-serif (like DM Sans or Inter) for body text.
- **Style:** Dark, premium, executive feel. Minimal text per slide — use visuals, icons, and short bullet points. No clip art. Think luxury financial brand, not tech startup.
- **Tagline:** "Wealth · Protection · Legacy" — use sparingly on title and closing slides.
- **Logo note:** The company logo is a gold eagle/phoenix on dark navy. Use a placeholder logo area on title and closing slides.

## TONE

Confident, empowering, direct. Speak TO the agents — "your portal," "your checklist," "built for you." This is a team rally, not a product demo. Make them feel invested in.
